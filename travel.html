5 Days | Culture & Wildlife Loop

Drive from Addis to Awash, explore Awash Falls, and relax at Filwoha hot springs.

Explore the 99 mosques of Harar Jugol, Rimbaud House, and experience Hyena Man feeding ceremony at night.

Drive back to Addis with birdwatching breaks along Rift Valley Lakes.

Ethiopian Endemics Bird Watching Tour

10 Days | Peak Birding Season: October to March

Ethiopia is a legendary birding haven hosting over 860 bird species, with 30+ endemic to the highland plateaus. Our specialized tour covers the highest diversity zones.

Top Hotspots Covered:

Bale Mountains National Park (Top 5 African birding zone - 310+ species), Simien Mountains (Lammergeyer), Rift Valley Lakes (pelicans, flamingos), Awash Park (bustards, bee-eaters).

Target Endemics & Rarities:

Prince Ruspoli's Turaco, Stresemann's Bush Crow, Abyssinian Catbird, Spot-breasted Lapwing, Rouget's Rail, Nechisar Nightjar.

Inclusions:

  • Premium accommodations
  • All meals & field beverages
  • Transportation via high-clearance 4x4
  • National park ranger & camping fees
  • Expert certified birding guide
